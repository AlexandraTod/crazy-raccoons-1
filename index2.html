About Us

Here at Humanitarian Organization Iskra, we are committed to a single goal; we strive to make everyday life obstacle free as much as possible for persons with cerebral palsy and their families. We are a private, non-profit organization registered under Macedonian law, dedicated to finding innovative ways to help those in need overcome their challenge.

Our funding comes specifically for a certain project from local companies willing to support us on a case by case basis. We do not have an endowment and we do not charge fees from our members.

projects

One at a time

Established a program with packages of basic food and hygienic supplies on a quarterly basis for economically disadvantaged families with a member that has a cerebral palsy.

Assistive Technology for Mobility

Project dedicated to offfering low cost renting services for assistive technologies such as lifts for at home use, ramps and adjusted door to door transport.

World CP Day

We are a part of a global network of nonprofits that celebrate this day in various ways to raise awareness and advocate for the rights of people wiht CP.
