About us

Here at Humanitarian Organization Iskra, we are committed to a single goal; we strive to make everyday life obstacle free as much as possible for persons with cerebral palsy and their families. Explore our website to learn more about all that we’re doing to make a change. We are a private, non-profit organization dedicated to finding innovative ways to help those in need overcome challenges, specifically disabled children and adults. Our focus is on improving the quality of life for children and adults with cerebral palsy and their immediate family.

FAQ

What is HO ISKRA?

We are a nonprofit registered under Macedonian law, with a mission to help marginalized groups in our society, recently with focus on people with CP, to lead a better life.

How are you supported financially?

Our funding comes specifically for a certain project from local companies willing to support us on a case by case basis. We do not have an endowment and we do not charge fees from our members.

Can i contribute to your work?

Of course you can! You can donate or volunteer, or just send us a shout out that you support our work.If you want to volunteer your time and help us with a task, you are welcome to express your interest by sending us an email, then filling out a form and start contributing immediately, no matter if you are in Macedonia or not. We encourage remote volunteers!
